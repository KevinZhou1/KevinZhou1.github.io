How Telemore Works

Telemore is designed to empower teams with actionable insights while maintaining simplicity. With a seamless setup, employees can quickly gain access to personalized productivity recommendations, and managers can view team trends at a glance. Our AI-powered platform transforms your team's workflows for better efficiency and focus.

  • Step 1: Install Telemore on employee devices with minimal effort.
  • Step 2: Real-time productivity data is collected and analyzed by our generative AI.
  • Step 3: Employees and managers receive actionable insights to improve workflow and collaboration.

Our Services

  • Employee Productivity Monitoring: Gain visibility into task progress, engagement, and application usage.
  • AI-Powered Insights: Receive daily summaries, task prioritization tips, and focus recommendations tailored to individual and team needs.
  • Hybrid Team Support: Ensure productivity and alignment across remote, hybrid, and in-office teams.

Our goal is to enhance your team's workflow while respecting privacy and minimizing distractions.

Seamless Integration

Telemore integrates effortlessly into your existing workflow with compatibility across:

  • Windows, macOS, and Linux operating systems.
  • Popular project management tools and productivity platforms.
  • Custom workflows, with optional API access for advanced use cases.

Setup is quick and intuitive, with no steep learning curve for managers or employees.

Security & Privacy

We understand that security and privacy are non-negotiable. Telemore is built with enterprise-grade protections, ensuring your team's data remains secure and private:

  • Data Encryption: End-to-end encryption (AES-256) safeguards all data.
  • Minimal Data Collection: Only task and application usage data is collected, avoiding personal or sensitive information.
  • Compliance: Fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other global privacy standards.

Transparency is at the core of our platform. Employees can view what’s being monitored and understand how it benefits their productivity.
