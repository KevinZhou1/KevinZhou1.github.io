Data Security

Telemore is built with security as a foundational pillar, ensuring that all collected data is protected at every step. We employ industry-leading security measures to safeguard your information:

  • End-to-End Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption to ensure it remains secure and inaccessible to unauthorized parties.
  • Secure Authentication: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) and strict access controls protect user accounts and administrative access.
  • ISO-Certified Practices: Telemore follows ISO 27001 standards to deliver world-class information security management.

Our robust infrastructure ensures that your team's data is always protected against breaches and vulnerabilities.

Minimal Data Collection

Telemore adheres to a privacy-first philosophy. We collect only the data necessary to deliver actionable productivity insights:

  • What We Collect: Task progress, application usage patterns, idle times, and engagement metrics.
  • What We Don’t Collect: Personal messages, email content, file data, or keystrokes. Telemore respects employee privacy by avoiding intrusive data collection practices.

This ensures that while we help teams stay productive, we protect individual privacy and personal information at all times.

Privacy and Transparency

We believe that trust is built through transparency. That’s why Telemore provides employees and managers with clear, accessible information on what data is being monitored and how it’s used:

  • Employee Dashboards: Employees can view their own productivity data and insights, ensuring clarity about what is being tracked.
  • Customizable Monitoring: Employers can adjust what is monitored to meet organizational and legal requirements.
  • Usage Policies: Our platform allows companies to define and share monitoring policies with their teams to align expectations and build trust.

Our goal is to ensure that productivity insights are delivered ethically and transparently.

Global Compliance

Telemore is fully compliant with major international data privacy regulations, ensuring your organization stays protected and legally compliant:

  • GDPR Compliance: Adheres to strict European Union regulations for data privacy and security.
  • CCPA Compliance: Meets California Consumer Privacy Act standards, ensuring transparency and control for users.
  • Regular Audits: Telemore undergoes regular security audits to maintain compliance with global standards.

Our commitment to compliance ensures that your data is handled with care, no matter where your team operates.

Data Retention and Deletion

Telemore offers customizable data retention policies to align with your organization’s needs. We only store data for as long as it’s needed to deliver productivity insights:

  • Retention Policies: Set retention periods that align with your internal guidelines or legal requirements.
  • Data Purging: Upon request, all data can be permanently deleted from our servers to ensure complete removal.
  • Ownership of Data: Your organization retains full ownership and control over its data at all times.

With Telemore, you remain in control of your data while ensuring compliance with retention policies.
